Cost: 2 DP

Encroching Swarm

Canoptek Scarabs can be taken in squads of 9/12 models.
The points cost of which is equal to the points cost of squads of (3 + 6) or (6 + 6) models.

Adittionally, scarabs in squads of 9/12 models gain the following rule:
- When a model in this unit is destroyed, roll a D6. On a 6+, one additional Canoptek Scarab is added to the unit.
